What Makes a Great Water Delivery Company?

1. Water Quality & Certification

All top providers should have:

  • HACCP Certification: International food safety standard
  • ESMA Approval: Emirates Authority for Standardization
  • ISO Certification: Quality management standards
  • Low Sodium: Typically under 20mg/L
  • Balanced Minerals: Calcium, magnesium, potassium

2. Delivery Service Quality

Look for companies offering:

  • Same-day or next-day delivery options
  • Free delivery across service areas
  • Flexible scheduling (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • Emergency delivery for urgent needs
  • Professional, courteous delivery staff

3. Customer Service

Premium providers offer:

  • Multiple ordering channels (phone, WhatsApp, online)
  • 24/7 customer support
  • Easy complaint resolution
  • Account management for businesses
  • Responsive communication
