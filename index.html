Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about water delivery in Dubai

How much does 5 gallon water delivery cost?

Pricing ranges from AED 6 to AED 10 per bottle depending on order quantity. Bulk orders (50+ bottles) get the best rates at AED 6-7 per bottle. All deliveries are FREE across Dubai, Sharjah, and Ajman.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! Order before 2 PM and receive your water the same day. We offer emergency delivery within 4 hours for urgent needs across all service areas in UAE.

Is the water safe and certified?

Absolutely! Our water is HACCP and ESMA certified, meeting international safety standards. It undergoes 12-stage purification with regular quality testing and government compliance.

What areas do you deliver to?

We provide free delivery across Dubai (all areas including Marina, Downtown, Silicon Oasis), Sharjah, Ajman, Umm Al Quwain, and Ras Al Khaimah with same-day service.

Do I need to pay a deposit for bottles?

Yes, there's a one-time refundable deposit of AED 15-25 per bottle. This deposit is fully refunded when you return the bottles. Bulk corporate orders may have deposit waived.

Can I schedule regular deliveries?

Yes! Set up automatic scheduled deliveries (daily, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly) based on your consumption. You can modify or pause anytime without penalties.
